Nida

When I arrived to Klaipeda (30th of August) I had my first trip with the Erasmus group. We went to Nida. We visited the place and enjoyed the beautiful views of the dunas and also, you can see Kaliningrado from there. We had lunch there and spent the day getting to know each other.

Palanga

During the second weekend in Klaipeda we visited Palanga. We already knew each other a little bit more and this trip was even more fun. We took some pictures, visited the city and its parks, beach and restaurants. We even had free time to explore by ourselves the city. Even if I would've loved to spend more time there, we had so much fun.
